CORE CONCEPTS
================================================================================

DELTA ENCODING
  Stock price changes are encoded as letters:
    a = -1% or less
    b = -0.5%
    c = -0.1%
    d = 0%
    e = +0.1%
    f = +0.5%
    g = +1% or more

WORDS
  Each "word" represents a portfolio's price movements in one time period:
    - Length = number of stocks
    - Characters = individual stock deltas
    - Example: "acgaeb" = 6 stocks with specific movements

MODEL TASK
  Input: sequence_length words (context window, default 16)
  Output: Predict the next word (next period's movements)
  Architecture: Transformer (GPT-2 style, 256 hidden, 6 layers, 8 heads)

VOCABULARY
  Number of unique words varies by data:
    - Usually 100-10,000 for typical datasets
    - Theoretical max with N stocks: 7^N
    - Actual data much smaller
