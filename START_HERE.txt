ğŸ“– KEY CONCEPTS (In 60 Seconds):

Delta: Percentage price change (-1% to +1%)
  Example: AAPL goes from $100 to $100.50 = +0.5% delta = letter 'f'

Word: Price changes for multiple stocks in one time period
  Example: "acgdef" = AAPL(-1%), AAL(-0.1%), MSFT(+1%), ...

Sequence: Series of words over time
  Input: "acgd"
  Output: Predict next word

Model: Transformer that learns to make this prediction
  Learns patterns in how stocks move together

Training: Adjusting model weights to minimize prediction error
  Goal: Minimize loss, maximize accuracy
